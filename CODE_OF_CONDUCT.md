# Code of Conduct

ShiftQuest contributors are expected to keep discussions welcoming, constructive and focused on the project.

- Treat people with respect regardless of experience, background or identity.
- Critique ideas and code, not individuals.
- Avoid harassment, discriminatory language, doxxing and unwanted contact.
- Do not share private user data, credentials or vulnerability details publicly.
- Assume good intent while remaining clear about technical and safety boundaries.

Unacceptable behavior may be removed and repeat participants may be blocked from project spaces. Concerns can be reported privately to the repository owner through GitHub.
